Research

I'm interested in applying deep learning technique for interdisciplinary research. Recently, I deployed the DGD model for the application of finding subtypes of cancers and predicting the possible cancer metastasis. When I was an undergraduate student, I first discovered the photoexcited audible sound effect and proposed a potential mechanisms. Besides, I also tasted computational chemistry, from material design to DFT-based computation.





Development of a deep generative model for cancer gene expression
Yan Li, Anders Krogh

This work demonstrates the multiple applications of the DGD model: exploring for cancer subtypes and predicting cancer metastasis.






Computational study of transition metal single-atom catalysts supported on nitrogenated carbon nanotubes for electrocatalytic nitrogen reduction
Yanyang Qin, Yan Li, Wenshan Zhao, Shenghua Chen, Tiantian Wu, Yaqiong Su

This work has provided a deeper insight into designing high-efficiency and stable NRR SACs supported by NCNTs.





Laser-induced photoexcited audible sound effect based on reticular 2-bromo-2-methylpropionic acid modified Fe3O4 nanoparticle aggregates
Yan Li, Hongrui Zhu, Junbo Duan, Youshen Wu, Daocheng Wu

Revealing and reporting the PEAS effect for the first time and proposing possible mechanisms.
